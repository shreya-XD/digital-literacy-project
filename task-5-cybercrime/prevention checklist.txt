Part B – Prevention Checklist


“Stay Safe Online” Checklist
* Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone 
* Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages 
* Always verify payment requests before approving 
* Use official apps for transactions only 
* Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible 
* Regularly check bank statements for unusual activity 
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions 
* Do not trust calls claiming to be from banks without verification 
?? UPI-Specific Tips 
* Remember: You do NOT need to enter a PIN to receive money 
* Never approve “collect request” unless you initiated it 
?? Reporting Information
* Report fraud at: https://cybercrime.gov.in 
* Call helpline: 1930
